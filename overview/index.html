Overview of TDC Datasets

TDC is a collection of machine learning tasks spread across different domains of therapeutics.

  • From bench to bedside: TDC covers a variety of learning tasks going from wet-lab target identification all the way to biomedical product manufacturing.
  • Most recent innovation: TDC is updated regularly to include the most recent datasets such as datasets on antibody therapeutics, gene-editing.
  • Machine-learning ready: TDC provides side information for many datasets. These feature information are carefully selected based on existing practice in machine learning.
TDC is an open-source effort. We welcome contributions from the research community. If you want to get involved, check out the contribution guide!

Learning Tasks in TDC

For each learning problem, TDC categorizes learning tasks into four therapeutics areas (i.e., Target Discovery, Activity, Efficacy and Safety, and Manufacturing) across two types of biomedical products (i.e., Small-Molecule Drugs and Biologics).

For each selection of (problem, area, product), TDC provides a number of learning tasks with associated datasets. To explore the datasets, click the tag of the task of interest in the following tables.
